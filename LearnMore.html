Biomes

The vast landscapes of Nigeria stretch for 923,786 km. In the South, you
can find landscapes such as tropical rainforests. As you travel North,
you will begin to see the makings of semi-desert regions, and the country houses
big rivers such as the Niger.

Ethnicities

Over 500 languages and cultures exist in Nigeria, the official language being English and the main languages being:

  1. Hausa (North)
  2. Yorabu (Southwest)
  3. Igbo (Southeast)
  4. Official language -
    1. English
