COOKIES

Our website uses a small amount of cookies to manage our pop-ups. Most of the pages on this website have pop-ups. We simply use cookies to recognise whether your browser has already seen one of these pop-ups so that your browser won't be shown this pop-up again within a month. Most of the pop-ups, other than the pop-up about cookies, tell the viewer how to unlock more features by signing up for an account. Our cookies cannot tell if the user already has an account, so , even if you have already signed up, you may see the pop-up again after a month. You do not have to sign up to avoid repetition of this pop-up, just closing the pop-up means that the cookies will recognise your browser. If updates are made to our cookies, this page will be updated.
